About DIRI BAKAL

Your one-stop destination for trendy and affordable fashion

Our Story

DIRI BAKAL was founded in 1870 di pa uso taho with a simple mission: to make fashion accessible and affordable without compromising on quality or style. What began as a small online boutique has grown into a global fashion destination loved by customers worldwide.

Our founder, Daryl Pasmala, identified a gap in the market for high-quality, on-trend fashion at reasonable prices. Drawing from his experience in the fashion industry, he created DIRI BAKAL to bridge this gap and provide customers with a curated collection of stylish pieces that don't break the bank.

Today, DIRI BAKAL operates in over 20 countries, with flagship stores in New York, London, Tokyo, and Sydney, alongside our ever-growing online presence.

Our Mission

At DIRI BAKAL, we believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best without spending a fortune. Our mission is to democratize fashion by offering trend-forward designs at accessible price points while maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices.

Quality

We rigorously test all our products to ensure they meet our high standards for durability and comfort.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing and eco-friendly practices.

Community

We value our global community of customers and strive to create inclusive collections for everyone.

Ethics

We ensure fair working conditions and wages throughout our supply chain.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At DIRI BAKAL, sustainability isn't just a buzzwordâ€”it's at the core of everything we do. We believe that fashion can be both stylish and responsible, and we're committed to making choices that benefit our planet and its people.

Materials

We prioritize sustainable materials like organic cotton, recycled polyester, and Tencel. By 2026, we aim to have 75% of our materials come from sustainable sources.

Production

We partner with factories that uphold fair labor practices and minimize environmental impact. Our production facilities use renewable energy where possible and implement water-saving technologies.

Packaging

We've eliminated single-use plastics from our packaging, opting instead for recycled and biodegradable materials. Our shipping boxes are made from 100% recycled cardboard.

Learn more about our sustainability journey and goals in our annual Sustainability Report.
